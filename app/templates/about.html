About

Purpose

- This site is intended for people who are shopping for homes in Chicago in order to track and compare properties they have seen (or wish to see).

Data Sources

- All data related to properties or other mapped elements is taken from publicly available data portals that are maintained by the city of Chicago and/or Cook County. They can be accessed here: Chicago Data Portal | Cook County Open Data

Estimated Taxes/Payments

- Property tax estimates are currently based on 2019 Board of Review final assessment amounts. This is due to the fact that assessments in many areas were lowered in 2020 for the purpose of Covid-19 relief and can be expected to return to something approaching or surpassing 2019 levels for 2021.
- The assessment amount is multiplied by the 2021 Illinois state equalization factor (currently 3.2234 - the highest since 2010 as a response to current inflation) and the most recent (2020) average property tax rate across Chicago (6.911%). A tax estimate for properties outside the city limits (but still within Cook County) might be considerably higher given the average rates in those areas (North/Northwest Suburbs: 9.048% and South/Southwest Suburbs: 11.822%). For instance, a property owner in the suburbs could reasonably expect to pay 30-70% more in annual taxes than a property owner in the city with similarly valued property. Reference here: Cook County Average Taxes Rates
- While pains are taken to seek accuracy in these amounts, all 2021/2022 data is not yet available, and all estimates should be considered exactly that - estimates.

Mortgage Calculator

- The built-in feature of this site which allows the user to estimate a monthly mortgage payment is only made available to those properties for which reliable data could be obtained from the Cook County Assessor's Office. In the event that no data was available, many mortgage calculators are available online, but a user would have to factor in his/her own HOA and tax estimates to get a complete payment picture.
Example here: Zillow Mortgage Calculator

Disclaimer

- Cook County property data on which this site is based is sometimes missing or incomplete from the public databases. In the event that data cannot be retrieved for a property here, it may be obtainable via more specific web searches or by contacting the county directly.
